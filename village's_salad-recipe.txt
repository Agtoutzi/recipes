Ingredients

    125 g greek feta
    2 green peppers
    1 cucumber
    1 small red onion
    5 ripe tomatoes
    1 handful of kalamata olives
    1 tesapoon dried oregano
    3-4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
    3-4 tablespoons red wine vinegar


Method

    Crumble the feta into chunks, deseed and slice the peppers, and peel and slice 
	the cucumber and onion. Slice the tomatoes.
    Combine all the ingredients except the oregano, oil and vinegar in a salad bowl.
    Just before serving, sprinkle the salad with the oregano, season with freshly 
	ground black pepper and dress with the oil and red wine vinegar.
